## Optimization Strategy

We stripped out all legacy CSS-in-JS libraries and replaced them with zero-runtime utility classes. This reduced the bundle size by 40%.

The rendering engine now utilizes a virtualized list for the log feed, allowing for thousands of entries without performance degradation.
